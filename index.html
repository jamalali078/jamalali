FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Assam Eduverse an official board website?

No. Assam Eduverse is an independent educational initiative and is not an official website of SEBA, AHSEC or CBSE. It is created to support students with notes, solutions and guidance.

Do I need to pay to use Assam Eduverse?

Many resources such as notes and solutions are free to access. If any paid features are added later, they will be clearly mentioned on the main website.

Can I rely only on Assam Eduverse for exams?

Assam Eduverse should be used as a supporting resource. Students are encouraged to use their official textbooks, teachers' guidance and previous year question papers along with these notes and solutions.
